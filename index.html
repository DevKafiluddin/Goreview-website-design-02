What Can Cause Liver Strain?

Modern lifestyles put enormous stress on your liver. Understanding the key risk factors is the first step toward protecting your most important detox organ.

Poor Diet

High sugar and processed foods overload the liver.

Alcohol

Even moderate drinking can stress liver cells over time.

Medications

Common OTC drugs can burden liver metabolism.

Environmental Toxins

Pollutants and chemicals require constant detoxification.

Sedentary Lifestyle

Lack of exercise contributes to fatty liver disease.

Obesity

Excess weight is a leading cause of non-alcoholic fatty liver.

Healthy vs. Strained Liver

See the clinical difference between a well-maintained liver and one under chronic stress.

Healthy Function

  • Efficient toxin removal
  • Balanced bile production
  • Stable energy levels
  • Clear skin & bright eyes
  • Healthy metabolism

Strained State

  • Sluggish detoxification
  • Digestive discomfort
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Skin problems & yellowing
  • Weight gain & bloating
